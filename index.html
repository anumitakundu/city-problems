Dharavi is a locality in Mumbai, Maharashtra. India, considered to be one of Asia's largest slums. The Dharavi slum was founded in 1884. Dharavi has suffered from many epidemics and other disasters. including a widespread plague in 1896, which killed over half of the population of Bombay. Dharavi is one of the most densely populated areas in the world.
